Tanks & Bunds

Bunds provide a 'second line of defence' for controlling the safe storage of hazardous liquids and chemicals as they form an enclosure that surrounds a tank. This provides the essential piece of mind should a tank have a catastrophic failure.

Our storage tanks would be manufactured using the latest welding equipment / technology. Working with thermoplastics such as, Polypropylene, HDPE and PVDF to name a few. These off the shelf materials are used as materials of construction, due to their high corrosion resistance and their stability over a wide range of temperatures. Tanks and bunds can be built to the latest standards, BS EN 12573, enabling them to store drinking water, acids/alkaline and foodstuff. With thermoplastic the list of media which can be stored in these tanks, is virtually endless.
